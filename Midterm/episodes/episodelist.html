About Episode Guide

Episodes

Episode 1: Self-Proclaimed Psychic: Reigen Arataka ~And Mob~

Espers fight with supernatural phenomena that can't be explained by science. Reigen Arataka, who runs the Spirits and Such Consultation Office, is one of those psychics--well, a fake one. His only hope is a dull middle schooler named Mob.

Episode 2: Doubts About Youth ~The Telepathy Club Appears~

The Telepathy Club wants Mob to join them to avoid being disbanded for not having enough members but Reigen wants Mob to focus on his job instead.

Episode 3: An Invite to a Meeting ~Simply Put, I Just Want to Be Popular~

During Body Improvement Club practice, Mob faints and is left in the club room to recover. The Telepathy Club comments that Mob will never be popular even if he trains his body.

Episode 4: Idiots Only Event ~Kin~

During the cold open, delinquents from Salt Middle School are in a turf war with a rival school. When the rival delinquents are unable to win, they call for their leader, Teruki "Teru" Hanazawa. In spite of being physically unimposing, Teru disposes of the Salt delinquents with ease.

Episode 5: Ochimusha ~Psychic Powers and Me~

Mob has promised not to use his powers ever again on humans, but a new psychic; Teru, appears. Will Mob use his powers or refuse the duel?

Episode 6: Discord ~To Become One~

The president of the Telepathy Club asks Mob to help her find other espers. And a mysterious man talks to Ritsu mistaking him for his older brother Mob.
