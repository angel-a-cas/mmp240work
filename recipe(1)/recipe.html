What we gon need for this sweet dominican treat

  1. Get those dough discs, Goya Empanda is what I usually pick
  2. Get Queso de frier (a white cheese for frying), Tropical is my go to
  3. Something to fry it in of course, Pan or Deep Fryer works well
  4. Oil to fry with
  5. a fork

Directions

  1. get your disc and lay it flat on anything, i like cutting boards but that's just personal preference
  2. get your frying cheese and either chop it up or hope you can fit it in
  3. like a sandwich get another disc on top
  4. with your fork you're going to start to press down on all around the edges
  5. once you repeat this process for how many you want start to prep the fryer
  6. Once that oil looks prepped I like to wait 2-5 minutes (depending on what im using)
  7. In a pan I like to do 5 minutes as I like to flip to make sure i get that golden brown. However I haven't done it much with a deep fryer so I'm guessing based of these results we put it for 3 minutes
